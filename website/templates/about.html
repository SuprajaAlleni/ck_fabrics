ABOUT US

CK Fabrics Private Limited Leading Manufacturers Suppliers, Exporters of PP SPUN BOND NON - WOVEN FABRIC since 2012 and connected with many reputed customers across south India. CK Fabrics is a Hyderabad based Non-woven fabric manufacturer, a one stop hub for all the non- woven fabric needs of clients in diverse industries such as packaging, medical and surgical, health & hygiene, mattress industry and agricultural purposes. Our Product includes of Melt Blown Non-Woven Fabric, Laminated Non-Woven Fabric, Functional Non-Woven Fabric Rolls & sheets, and Hydrophilic Non-Woven Fabric. Entire production is carried out at our plant and is monitored at all stages by our quality team.
